Request Your Decision Record

This sends a request for a summary of your CraftBeacon coaching sessions — a record you can keep for your own files.

What's included:

  • A short headline and 2–3 sentence recap for each coaching session
  • Which coaching lane each session covered
  • The date of each session

What's not included:

  • Full conversation transcripts
  • CraftBeacon's coaching responses — only your side of each session is summarized

Your records are kept for as long as you're a member, so there's no need to request one after every session — only when you actually need one.

Once you confirm, your request goes to CraftBeacon's team and your report will be emailed to you.

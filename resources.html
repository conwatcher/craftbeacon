ISBN registration

An ISBN is required for most retail and library distribution. Platform-assigned free ISBNs limit portability and imprint control — understand the tradeoffs before choosing.

Official
Bowker / MyIdentifiers
The official U.S. ISBN agency. Best option when you want your own imprint. Verify current package pricing.
Platform ISBN
KDP Free ISBN
Free ISBN for KDP print books. Imprint appears as "Independently published." Limited to KDP use.
Platform ISBN
IngramSpark Free ISBN
Free ISBNs for U.S. self-publishers. Imprint uses IngramSpark branding and is limited to their platform.

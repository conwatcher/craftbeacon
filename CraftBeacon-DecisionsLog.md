**DECISION: Welcome email delivered via Outseta drip campaign, not broadcast or transactional email.**
Date: June 16, 2026
Status: PERMANENT — this is the correct mechanism for automated welcome emails in Outseta.
Background: The June 9 welcome send was a one-time manual broadcast to the test account only. New subscribers were not receiving the welcome email. Eight people signed up between June 9 and June 16 with no welcome.
Resolution: Built a drip campaign called "Welcome Email" triggered on subscription to the CraftBeacon Subscribers mailing list, sender Patrick (patrick@thecraftbeacon.com), immediate delay, Simple Text template with lighthouse logo header. Activated with "Start drip to existing users" checked, sending to all existing subscribers immediately.
Why this matters: Any future email sequences — onboarding, nurture, newsletter — should also use drip campaigns, not broadcasts, to ensure automatic delivery to new subscribers.

**DECISION: CraftBeacon lighthouse logo uploaded to Outseta organization Profile.**
Date: June 16, 2026
Status: PERMANENT — this is the source for the email header logo.
Background: Previous welcome email showed a default circular grid placeholder icon because no logo had been uploaded. The logo source was not locatable in Design settings or Email settings across multiple sessions.
Resolution: The organization Profile is accessed by clicking "The Craft Beacon" account name in the top left of the Outseta dashboard. Uploading the lighthouse logo image there populates the email header across all outgoing emails.

Date: June 16, 2026

The coaching philosophy

The AI that coaches
instead of writing

Every AI writing tool on the market will write for you. CraftBeacon is built on a different belief: that the author's own voice, instincts, and creative decisions are the thing worth protecting.

When something in your manuscript isn't working, you don't need a replacement — you need to understand why it's broken and where the fix lives. That's what CraftBeacon does.

Other AI tools

Generate prose, dialogue, scenes, and blurbs. Hand you finished text you didn't write and have to live with.

CraftBeacon

Diagnose the root cause. Ask the question that opens your thinking. Return you to your own creativity with direction and momentum.
